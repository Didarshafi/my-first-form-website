Nature of our Bangladesh

Published on14 january,2025

Seasonal country

Bangladesh is known as the "Land of Six Seasons" because of its distinct seasons, which are based on temperature, rainfall, and wind
The six seasons are: Summer (Grisma): The warmest season, which takes place in the months of Baishakh and Jaistha
Rainy season (Barsa): The wettest season, which takes place from June to October
Autumn (Sarat): The season that takes place after the rainy season
Late autumn (Hemanta): The season that takes place in the months of Kartik and Agrahayan
Winter (Shitt): The coldest season, which takes place in the months of Pous and Magh Spring (Basanta): The season that takes place in the months of Falgoon and Chaitra
